Background

Academic performance is the measure of a student’s achievements in various subjects (Academic Performance - Ballotpedia, n.d.). This impacts a student’s future prospects and opportunities. What’s intriguing is the connection of academic performance to personal life.

As students, we have personal experiences that suggest that factors in our personal life can shape our academic outcomes. However, understanding the relationship of academic performance and personal life is calling for a systematic investigation.

Objectives

Our objective is to explore the connection between personal life and academic performance. In particular, we will determine whether a student’s relationship with their parents and their relationship status has a direct influence on their academic performance. We measure academic performance through the General Weighted Average (GWA) from last school year (S.Y. 2022-2023) and the number of times the students made it into the Director’s List (DL), which is a very commendable achievement.
